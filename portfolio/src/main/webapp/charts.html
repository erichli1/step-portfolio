Program Efficacy

Suppose you have $100 to donate to charity, and you want to increase the amount of time girls in East Africa spend in school. There are four charities, each of which do one of the following. Which one do you think will result in the most additional number of years of schooling per $100?


Show answer
The most effective program is educating the parents about the benefits of education. This research study was conducted by the Jameel Poverty Action Lab, an MIT-based research organization that was founded by 2019 Economics Nobel Prize winners Abhijit Banerjee and Esther Duflo.

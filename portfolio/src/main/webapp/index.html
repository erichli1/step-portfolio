My work

Service


After the devastating 2008 earthquake in Sichuan, China, my sisters and I knew we had to do something, do anything. We went door-to-door in our neighborhood the next day and raised more than $80, an unthinkable amount of money for my 7 year old self. More than a decade later, our initial efforts to fundraise have evolved into the 501c3 non-profit We Care Act, deidcated to engaging youth in community service. Currently, our primary project is a digital literacy initiative (video above) which aims to refurbish and recycle electronics for the dual purpose of increasing social mobility and protecting the environment.

Technology


The research organization Opportunity Insights has shown that the neighborhood that somebody grows up in has vast implications for the socioeconomic status of that person. With this in mind, I helped develop ECHO, an affordable housing search website that focuses on neighborhoods first, and then listings. By using the user's preferences, ECHO generates neighborhood recommendations and then displays transit and listing options given the user's budget. Such software will lower the barriers of information regarding neighborhood quality to ensure that low-income residents in the Boston area have access to high socioeconomic mobility neighborhoods.

Consulting


Global Research and Consulting is a student-run nonprofit consulting group dedicated to making high-impact positive social change. The organization has four main branches: the Specialist High Impact Program (develop an M&E system for nonprofits), Consulting (increase the efficacy of nonprofits), Tech and Analytics (provide tech solutions to amplify impact of nonprofits), and Insights Research (publish think tank articles to make progress on global issues). With over 100 members across 6 branches, GRC has worked with the World Bank and the World Wildlife Fund among others to create change. As CTO of the organization, I create and execute the technology strategy for GRC's operations and integrate technology into the fabric of our work to help us better help others.
